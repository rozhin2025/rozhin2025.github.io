Experiences

I started my internship at 6thSolution in July 2016, working as a software developer for a year. My responsibilities included software design and development, programming and implementation, and conducting research in computer science and engineering. During this time, I contributed to projects such as Apex NLP and Smart Calendar, where I utilized NLP to manage events and daily activities.

In August 2018, I began working at the Sharif Creativity and Entrepreneurship Development Office as a Technical Specialist. I contributed to the Sharif Star project, gaining valuable experience in project management and web development. In this role, I assessed and troubleshot technical programs, created user-friendly interfaces, and optimized performance to enhance user experience and engagement.

Alongside my master's studies, I began working in Germany in 2020 as a student intern software developer at Tideways GmbH for nearly three years. My primary responsibilities included converting existing JavaScript code to TypeScript and developing new features for the Tideways application. I successfully converted over 100 React components and tested them using Jest and snapshot testing. My role involved both front-end and back-end development, utilizing React and integrating with PHP and Symfony, as well as optimizing database communication with MySQL and SQLite.

My first research experience was at the University of Bonn in 2021, where I worked as a research assistant in the DFG Cluster of Excellence PhenoRob at the Institute of Crop Science and Resource Conservation for six month. My tasks included collecting and analyzing image and audio data from the field, developing and implementing algorithms for audio and image processing, and creating machine learning models for detecting and classifying bees and flowers. I used Python, OpenCV, TensorFlow, and Keras for this work. Through this role, I gained valuable experience in researching and developing new algorithms and models in audio data processing, machine learning, and other computer science and crop science topics.
